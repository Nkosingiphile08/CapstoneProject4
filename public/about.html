About Us

Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their health by providing a user-friendly platform for predicting, monitoring and managing hypertension.

We understand the importance of keeping track of your hypertension or blood pressure. Hypertension is a significant risk factor for various health conditions, including heart disease and stroke. Regular monitoring can help you make informed decisions about your health and work with healthcare professionals to manage your blood pressure effectively.

Our application allows you to:

  • -To make record of your hypertension status and track your blood pressure readings over time.
  • -Set medication reminders to ensure you stay on top of your treatment plan.
  • -Book your preferable doctor's appointment
  • -Access our community status status where you can post and interact with other people about your hypertension journey
  • -Access valuable information and resources about hypertension management and healthy lifestyle choices.

We are committed to providing a reliable and user-friendly experience, and we are always working to improve our app to better serve your needs.
